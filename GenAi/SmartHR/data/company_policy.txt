COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT

1. LEAVE POLICY
   Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave annually.
   Leave must be applied at least 3 days in advance unless it is an emergency.
   Sick leave requires a medical certificate if it exceeds 2 consecutive days.
   Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.

2. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
   Employees are allowed to work from home up to 2 days per week.
   Manager approval is required for additional remote work days.
   Employees must be available during standard working hours (9 AM to 6 PM).

3. WORKING HOURS
   Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
   Employees are expected to log a minimum of 8 hours per day.
   Late login beyond 10:00 AM must be approved by the reporting manager.

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
   Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
   Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct will not be tolerated.
   Violation of company policies may result in disciplinary action.

5. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION
   Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.
   Employees are evaluated based on performance, teamwork, and communication.
   Bonuses and promotions are based on performance ratings.

6. DATA SECURITY POLICY
   Employees must not share confidential company data with unauthorized individuals.
   All work-related data must be stored securely in company-approved systems.
   Use of personal devices for sensitive work is discouraged.

7. IT USAGE POLICY
   Company systems are to be used strictly for professional purposes.
   Downloading unauthorized software is prohibited.
   All internet usage may be monitored for security purposes.

8. GRIEVANCE HANDLING
   Employees can report concerns to HR confidentially.
   All grievances will be addressed within 7 working days.
   No retaliation will be taken against employees raising concerns.

END OF DOCUMENT
